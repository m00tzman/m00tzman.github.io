ABOUT

The developer:

First and foremost - Software is expression. This is an ethos that has stuck with me from a very young age. As an adolecent on the web in the early 2000's I have been exposed to many works, all of which an extension of their creators. As it was a nascent time in the history of the web these works had to work with, and overcome a variety of limitations in unorthodox and inventive ways. It is my firm belief that the software we create is an extension of ourselves, our clients, our culture - whether it be from a functional or design point of view.

My facisnation lies in human-software interaction, as well as the creation of tactile and accessible interfaces. Throughout my career, a common thread has been addressing the needs and capablilities of different audiences - whether it be for small local markets, or large education software platforms created for users with a large skill disparty. I want to continue to refine my skills and create new methods of interaction that engage the end user.

The person:

I am a lover of music with moody synthesizers, stories about robots, long distance running (preferrably in a place with a view), tiny dogs that look like mops, hyper-referential humor, and trying to integrate cayenne pepper into everything I cook.

You can most likely find me at a local arcade looking for a Tetris - The Grand Master cabinet.

I recieved a B.A. in New Media from SUNY Purchase, as well as attended New York Code And Design Academy as a part of their Web Development 100, Ruby On Rails, and Advanced Front End courses.
